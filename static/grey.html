About this site

This demonstration does not store number plates or precise locations. Spam content is also filtered out in the image processing step.

Detected geolocation and number plate could be collected and processed by a competent authority e.g. to inform the owners of the vehicles of potential problems with their vehicles - many drivers are unaware.

This site currently only shows them to the user who submits the photo, to show how the site functions. Number plates and faces are blocked out from submitted photos before being stored, and geolocation is trucated to tenths of a degree - a few miles square in the UK.
